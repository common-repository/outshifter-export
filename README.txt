Export products easily from your WooCommerce to Reachu.

== Description ==

Reachu lets you synchronize product information, orders and inventory across multiple stores and platforms. 

By connecting your store with Reachu you can easily synchronize inventory and product information across multiple stores, platforms, and applications. Reachu automates the connection process and management, making it easier to work with more partners, dropshippers and storefronts. While our technology ensures product information and inventory is up to date and orders flow back to your current store.

=== Key Features ===

• Automate order management from multiple sources.
 
• Synchronize inventory and stock across your ecosystem.

• Share products and collections with your partners.

• Easily manage cross-store sales partnerships, retailers and dropshippers.

• Up to date product information across platforms and applications.

• Rest API to build awesome custom experiences.

== Additional Information ==

This plugin relies on the Reachu service to import and manage products. The service is used to retrieve product information from external sources and synchronize it with the WooCommerce store.

In order to use this plugin, users must create a Reachu account and provide their API key in the plugin settings. This API key will be used to authenticate and connect to the Reachu service. 

To learn more about Reachu, please visit our website at https://reachu.io/.

Reachu's terms of use and privacy policies can be found at https://reachu.io/terms-of-service/ and https://reachu.io/privacy-policy/, respectively.
